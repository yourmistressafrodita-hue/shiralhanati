About

Designing online stores that respect both your customer and your bottom line.

I’m Shir Alhanati, an e‑commerce designer & developer who builds online stores that actually sell. I help product‑based brands turn casual visitors into confident buyers with conversion‑driven UX, sharp messaging and clean, fast development.

Design · UX · Development
For the past years, I’ve been working at the intersection of design, UX and development. I partner with founders and small to mid‑size brands who are ready to treat their online store as a real sales channel – not just a pretty brochure.

Instead of handing your project off to three different people, you get one partner who cares about the full journey: strategy, user experience, visual design and the technical implementation that makes it all feel seamless.

Why clients work with me
Strategic, not just aesthetic
I don’t design for “pretty”. Every layout, section and piece of microcopy is tied to a clear goal – more people understanding your product, trusting your brand and clicking “add to cart”.
UX that feels natural
Good e‑commerce UX should almost disappear. I design stores that are easy to browse, compare and buy from – especially on mobile, where most of your traffic actually lands.
Design that fits your brand
There is no one universal template. Together, we shape a visual language that feels like you – typography, color, product layout and the way we tell your story.
Development you don’t have to babysit
From Shopify and WordPress to custom front‑end builds, I develop your store to be fast, responsive and manageable, so you can keep things updated without fighting the tech.
My path into e‑commerce
I didn’t come from a world of pure “art for art’s sake”. My background combines graphic design, UX and economics, so I naturally think in terms of user behavior, incentives and the bottom line.

Over time, I saw the same pattern again and again: beautiful online stores that simply didn’t convert. That’s when I decided to focus on e‑commerce – designing experiences that respect both the customer’s time and the brand’s business goals.

Today, every project is a collaboration. You bring your product expertise, I bring the UX, design and development – and together we build a store that feels good to use and makes it easier for people to say “yes”.
How we’ll work together
Step 1
Discovery & strategy
We start with a focused conversation about your business, your customers and what “success” means for this project. From there we map the structure of the site, key pages and the main user journeys we need to support.
Step 2
UX & content structure
I create wireframes that define the layout, hierarchy and messaging blocks for your store – from homepage and collection pages to product pages, cart and checkout.
Step 3
Visual design
Once the structure is clear, we translate it into a visual system: typography, colors, components and interactions that match your brand and keep the experience consistent across devices.
Step 4
Development & launch
I build your store on the right platform, optimize performance and connect the tools you rely on – analytics, email, payment and shipping. We launch only after testing the critical flows.
